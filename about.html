ABOUT US

Sowing Seeds of Faith Foundation Inc is a non-profit organization designed to uplift the community by providing entertainment, food and clothing drives, along with other outreach programs.

This organization is committed to building stronger communities and neighborhoods through active community involvement and engagement for our youth and elderly population. The mission of the Sowing Seeds of Faith Foundation is to cultivate low-income communities by connecting them to skills, information, and services required to build a successful life.
